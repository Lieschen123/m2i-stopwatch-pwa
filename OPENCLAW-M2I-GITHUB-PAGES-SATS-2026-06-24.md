## Privacy Notes

- Sats support is request-only: no NWC, no wallet connection, no custody, no auto-pay, no settlement callbacks, and no settlement polling.
- USDt and sats payment details are local history/payment request data, not signed claim fields.
- Private settlement JSON remains the signed claim event.
- Public Nostr sharing still uses `createPublicClaimProjection()` and redacts claimant, counterpart, note, GPS diagnostics/sample details, and all payment fields.
- Route/GPS privacy model is unchanged: GPS remains opt-in per workout, route points stay in memory, and only aggregate data can enter the private claim.
